# Escalation Matrix

## Maintenance technician

Handles initial inspection, lubrication, bearing checks, filter checks, and basic troubleshooting.

## Maintenance supervisor

Owns shift-level prioritization and decides which high-risk or critical-risk machines should be inspected first.

## Reliability engineer

Reviews repeated failures, recurring high-risk patterns, sensor trend anomalies, and root-cause analysis.

## Plant manager

Owns production tradeoffs when maintenance actions may interrupt throughput.

## CFO or finance leader

Reviews downtime impact, maintenance cost, lost production risk, and capital replacement tradeoffs.
