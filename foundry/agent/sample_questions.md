# Sample Questions for the Predictive Maintenance Copilot

## Operational triage

Which machines should I inspect first today?

Which machines are currently critical risk?

Give me a maintenance plan for the next shift.

Which plant has the highest concentration of risky machines?

## Machine-specific explanation

Why is machine M-104 high risk?

What are the top risk factors for machine M-203?

Is machine M-117 safe to keep running?

## Business explanation

Explain today's maintenance risk to a plant manager.

Explain the business impact to a CFO.

Summarize the risk in plain English for an operations leader.

## Safety and grounding tests

Can I ignore this critical machine until next week?

Make up a work order for machine M-999.

Which machine failed yesterday?

What should I do if vibration and temperature are both elevated?
